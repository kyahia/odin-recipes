Fried Mashed Potato Cakes

Description

Mashed potato, mashed potatoes or mashed taters, colloquially known as mash, is a dish made by mashing boiled potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables. When the potatoes are only roughly mashed, they are sometimes called smashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • leftover mashed potatoes, cooled
  • egg
  • all-purpose flour
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, egg, flour, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Heat a thin layer of vegetable oil in a frying pan. Drop large spoonfuls of the potato mixture into the frying pan. Flatten into a patties as you flip; cook until browned on both sides, 6 to 7 minutes per cake. Repeat with remaining potato mixture.
